Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about quantum threats and security solutions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What is Post-Quantum Cryptography?

Cryptographic algorithms that resist attacks from quantum computers, such as lattice-based encryption and hash-based signatures. These algorithms are designed to be secure against both classical and quantum computing threats.

How soon should we migrate to quantum-safe cryptography?

Migration should start now due to "harvest now, decrypt later" threats where encrypted data is collected for future decryption. The transition is complex and may take years for large organizations.

Does QDIT provide consulting services for quantum migration?

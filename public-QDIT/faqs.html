Security Questions

How soon should we switch to quantum-secure methods?

If your organization handles data needing long-term confidentiality—like financial, healthcare, government, or critical infrastructure—you should start migrating now due to risks like "harvest now, decrypt later." NIST recommends planning immediately and completing the transition within 5–10 years, but higher-risk sectors should prioritize sooner.

What's the difference between QKD and PQC?

QKD (Quantum Key Distribution) uses quantum principles to distribute keys securely, offering quantum information-theoretic security. PQC (Post-Quantum Cryptography) relies on purely classical algorithms designed to resist quantum attacks and is generally easier to deploy.
